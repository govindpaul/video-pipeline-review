# Story #181-R: The Origami Tipper

**Genre:** Urban / Warmth
**Duration:** 18s
**Theme:** A subway busker discovers a commuter has been secretly leaving tips as origami animals.

---

## Story Summary
A violin busker opens his case to find coins and one paper origami crane among them, looks up, and spots the same commuter who always pauses to listen — now walking away with a slight smile.
