# Story #180-R: The Recurring Bear

**Genre:** Urban Mystery / Warmth
**Duration:** 18s
**Theme:** A taxi driver keeps finding the same lost teddy bear from different passengers.

---

## Story Summary
A taxi driver finds a worn teddy bear on his backseat, returns it to a passenger, then finds the same bear again from a completely different passenger — three times.

---

## Character DNA Blocks
[DRIVER_DNA]: male, 50, dark skin, square jaw, short grey hair, warm eyes, yellow taxi uniform shirt no text no logos, tired but kind expression

## Location DNA Blocks
[TAXI_LOC]: taxi interior, rearview mirror, black leather backseat, dashboard with meter, city lights through windows, evening

## Object DNA Blocks
[BEAR_OBJ]: small worn brown teddy bear, missing left ear, stitched belly patch, button eyes, 20cm tall

---

Finally, he places it on the passenger seat beside him and turns back to the wheel.

Night city curb, wide shot at street level. The taxi is stopped with the driver leaning out his window. Subsequently, a woman in a coat takes the teddy bear from his hand with a confused smile. ### Scene 3: Second Find (3s, trim: 500ms)

**Scene Purpose:** Different passenger, same bear appears again. The mystery deepens.

**Image Prompt:**
Subject: taxi driver staring at the same brown teddy bear on backseat, expression shocked | Pose: fully turned around in seat, both hands on headrest, mouth slightly open | Camera: close-up from backseat angle, 85mm, f/2.8, shallow DoF | Environment: taxi backseat, different time — morning sunlight through windows | Lighting: bright morning light from right window | Mood: disbelief, photograph

**Video Prompt:**
Morning taxi interior, close-up from backseat. The driver turns around completely and stares at the brown teddy bear sitting on the leather seat. Subsequently, he picks it up and holds it close to his face, examining the missing left ear. Then, he looks at the empty backseat, then back at the bear. Finally, he shakes his head slowly, brow furrowed, and places the bear on the dashboard next to his meter.

**Video Prompt:**
Rainy night taxi interior, medium close-up. The driver holds the teddy bear in both hands at chest level, staring at it with wide eyes. Subsequently, he looks up at the rearview mirror as if checking who just left. Then, he looks at the rain-streaked window where his last passenger walked away. Finally, he looks back at the bear and slowly starts to smile — accepting the mystery rather than solving it.
